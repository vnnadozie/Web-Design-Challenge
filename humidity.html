Humidity and Latitude






The cities have varied humidity even at the same latitude. One notable thing is that the variation in humidity decreases at latitudes from -20 to 0 degrees and 40 to 75 degrees. While this is common within the northern hemisphere, such clusters become rarer as the latitude decreases from the range mentioned above.
